Eleiko calibrated plates
Our selection of calibrated steel Eleiko power lifting plates is extensive. For ease of use and recognition, they are designed and coloured in accordance with the Global Power Lifting Alliance and the International Power Lifting Federation. Plates weighing from 500 grams to 25 kilograms plates are available. Fractional plates are necessary for your personal best or pre-competition lifts because they allow you to make incremental improvements to your power lifting regimens while remaining confident that the weight you are lifting is what is on the bar.
